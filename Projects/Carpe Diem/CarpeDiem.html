Misty Mountains


The towering mountains above me roar with wind. Snow flies off and drifts lazily, caught in the mountain's blizzard. The sun shines, but does nothing to warm the bitter cold. The air is dry as I capture the photo and escape to my cabin thousands of feet below.

The Heavens Above


A night canvas painted with stars looms over the snowy peaks. Jagged rocks peek out of their blanket of snow and point to the heavens in awe. The sky holds down the summit it sits upon. How far away are the stars we see? The distance seperating us can never be braved.

The Earth


How fragile our planet is. A thin blue line is the only thing seperating us from an empty vacumn. Yet we wage war on one another, although we are only torturing our fragile planet. We all come from the same place.
