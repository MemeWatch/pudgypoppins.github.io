Who We Are

Naan for the Poor is a non-profit dedicated to providing naan bread across the world. We deliver this tasty treat through helicopters, trucks, and people just like you. People across the world rely on Naan for the Poor for their naan needs.

The Four Step Process

Grandmas bake the naan using our secret recipe. In order to be productive, they must bake a thousand naan breads a day. You can help us out by donating a grandma.

Who We Help

Not Doctors Without Borders

We help people who lack the financial support that is needed to buy luxuries such as naan. Whether it is in third world countires, or one of your neighbors down the street, Naan for the Poor helps everyone.

How You Can Help

Donate a Granny

Grannies are a much needed resource in the naan business. Their participation is vital to our success, as without them, no naan could be baked. Due to how hard it is to accumulate a grandma, we are lacking the help we need in order to make naan. Help us out by donating a granny today!

Donate a Granny

Make Naan for Us

If you lack a granny, but are an avid baker yourself, you can support us by baking naan. Signing up allows access to our top secret recipe as well as other benefits such as: Discount on naan ingredients, and a warm gooey feeling inside that tells you that you are doing the right thing.

Make Naan for Us

Become a Trucker

Are you currently a truck driver, or want to be one? Do you like the idea of a cool radio handle? If so, this is the right job for you. However, it's not just truck driving. It is your duty to make sure the naan is safley delivered across borders. Should anyone try to take the naan, you must take them out.

Become a Truck Driver

Become a Pilot

Pilots will either fly in helicopters or planes depending on the distance. When flying domestic, you'll be in a helicopter. If overseas, you'll be flying a stealth bomber. Once over a target you'll release the naan, bringing joy to all. It is your duty to protect the aircraft against outside threats.

Become a Pilot
